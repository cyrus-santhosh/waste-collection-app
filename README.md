## 📖 Overview

**SmartBin** is a waste collection app that puts you in control of your waste disposal. Instead of being tied to fixed municipal schedules, you can check the fill levels and availability of smart waste bins near you — and dispose whenever it's convenient for you.

No more missed timings. No more overflowing bins. Just smarter, flexible waste disposal for everyone.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📍 **Locate Nearest Smart Bins** — Find smart waste bins near your location on an interactive map
- 📊 **Real-Time Fill Level Monitoring** — See how full each bin is before you make the trip
- 🔔 **Smart Notifications** — Get alerts when a nearby bin is emptied and ready
- 🕐 **Flexible Disposal Scheduling** — No fixed timings — dispose whenever works for you
- ♻️ **Waste Category Filters** — Find bins for specific waste types (general, recyclable, organic, e-waste)
- 📜 **Disposal History** — Track your past disposals and your environmental contribution
- 🌍 **Eco Impact Dashboard** — See stats on how much waste you've diverted from landfills
